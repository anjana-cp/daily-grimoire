# daily-grimoire
A daily planner disguised as a grimoire. Schedule your hours, track your deeds, and conquer the day in medieval style.

Update: Quests- A habit is not built in a day, but in the faithful repetition of deeds. Choose a Quest and record thy recurring deed in the grimoire and mark each step as it is taken. Fill every milestone to complete the quest, the habit forged.

Update: Level Up- Quests fulfill goals, complete sufficient quests and thy bingo squares mark themselves. Align a row, a column, or a diagonal across the tome and claim the reward inscribed in the centre — for those who persist long enough shall level up.
